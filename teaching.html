Teaching & Talks

In the Classroom & at the Podium

Two national MOOCs, 8+ courses taught, seven laboratories established — and a growing record of keynotes and invited lectures across three continents.

Teaching Keynotes Expert & Guest Lectures
01 / Teaching

Undergraduate, postgraduate, and MOOC delivery.

  • Undergraduate Teaching

    Developed and taught 6+ undergraduate courses across electronics, microelectronics, electrical engineering, signal processing, data communications, and solid-state devices — covering both theory and lab components during the Jaipur tenure.

  • Postgraduate Teaching

    Mentored postgraduate students at IIT Kharagpur and University of Cambridge across IoT, UAV systems, and digital manufacturing topics; supervised master's dissertations and project work.

  • MOOC Teaching

    Co-developed and co-delivered two national-scale MOOCs on IoT and Industrial IoT / Industry 4.0, reaching thousands of learners and adopted in engineering curricula across India.

  • Courses Organised

    Organised and ran 12+ industry- and academia-facing workshops on IoT, sensor networks, UAVs, and underwater systems across the IIT Kharagpur and University of Cambridge tenures.

  • Curriculum Development

    Contributed to curriculum development for the Department of Electronics & Communications Engineering at UEM Jaipur, including the establishment of seven new laboratories for teaching and research.

  • Co-authored Textbooks

    Co-authored Introduction to IoT (Cambridge University Press, 2020) and Introduction to Industrial Internet of Things and Industry 4.0 (CRC Press, 2021) — used as curricular references.

03 / Lectures

Expert and guest lectures.

Invited lectures at universities, research institutes, and industry forums across the UK, India, and beyond — covering digital twins, low-cost industrial digitalisation, IoT for constrained environments, and applied machine learning for complex systems.

Topics Covered in Invited Lectures

  • Digital Twins for Healthcare & Manufacturing

    Service-oriented, modular twin architectures; hospital and factory deployments.

  • Low-Cost Industrial Digitalisation

    The Shoestring philosophy; democratising access for SMEs in the UK, Australia, and India.

  • IoT for Constrained Environments

    Edge intelligence, UAV swarms, and resilient networking under resource scarcity.

  • Applied ML for Complex Systems

    Anomaly detection, predictive maintenance, domain adaptation, and privacy implications.

Across keynotes, expert lectures, and guest talks — spanning the UK, India, and virtual international forums.

LoDiSA Workshop Series

Founder and Organising Chair of the Low-Cost Digital Solutions for Industrial Automation (LoDiSA) workshop series — 2023, 2024, 2025. A global scientific forum attracting researchers from 20+ universities across 8 countries.
