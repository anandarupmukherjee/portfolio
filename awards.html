Awards & Honors

A Decade of Recognition

From the Gandhian Young Technology Innovation Award (awarded by the President of India) to Elsevier's Top 2% Scientists — a record of international recognition across engineering, IoT, and digital transformation research.

2025

  • Successfully accredited as Chartered Engineer (C.Eng.) — Engineering Council & IET, United Kingdom.
  • Selected as Postdoctoral Research Associate (PdRA), St. Edmund's College, University of Cambridge, United Kingdom.

2024

  • Listed among Elsevier's Top 2% Scientists (2024): one of 13 under Networking and Telecommunications, one of 3 under Mechanical Engineering and Transports, one of 16 under Information and Communication Technologies — out of 850 scientists selected from the University of Cambridge.
  • Elevated to IEEE Senior Member (IEEE Communications Society).

2023

  • Re-selected as Postdoctoral Research Associate (PdRA), Trinity Hall, University of Cambridge, United Kingdom.
  • Finalist, CPH Early Career Researcher Competition — "From SMEs to Public Health: Transitioning Digitalization from Manufacturing to Healthcare", Cambridge Public Health Showcase, St. John's College, Cambridge.
  • Semi-finalist, Chris Abel Postdoctoral Business Competition — "Digital Manufacturing on a Shoestring", University of Cambridge.
  • Best Conference Paper Award, IEEE ComSoc E-Health Technical Committee — "Reconfigure and Reuse: Interoperable Wearables for Healthcare IoT", IEEE INFOCOM 2020, USA.

2022

  • Selected as Postdoctoral Research Associate (PdRA), Trinity Hall, University of Cambridge, United Kingdom.

2021

  • Top 50 Most Popular Papers, IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS) — two papers recognised: "Timed Loops for Distributed Storage in Wireless Networks" and "Blockchain at the Edge: Performance of Resource-Constrained IoT Networks".

2020

  • Student Travel Grant, IEEE INFOCOM — only 10 selected globally.
  • ACM-India / IARCS Travel Grant for IEEE INFOCOM.

2019

  • Top Downloaded Article (2017–2018), Wiley Interdisciplinary Reviews — Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery.
  • Young Achiever Award, Institute of Scholars (InSc), Bangalore, India.

2018

  • Gandhian Young Technology Innovation Award for Socially Relevant Innovation — awarded by the President of India, New Delhi.
  • Dr. Amulya K. N. Reddy Award, Hari-om Ashram Prerit Society, New Delhi, India.
  • Fully funded conference attendee and presenter, IEEE WCNC 2018 — IIT Kharagpur Best Conference Grant.
  • Workshop speaker, Indo-UK (UGC-UKIERI) Joint Research Project — fully funded to attend workshop at City, University of London, United Kingdom for a week.

2017

  • Workshop organiser, Indo-UK (UGC-UKIERI) Joint Research Project — fully funded to organise and attend an Indo-UK joint project workshop on Internet of Things at C.V. Raman College of Engineering, Bhubaneshwar, India.
  • Workshop attendee, Indo-UK (UGC-UKIERI) Joint Research Project — fully funded for a week at Loughborough University London, United Kingdom.

2016

  • Fully funded participant, Microsoft-ACM Academic Research Summit, Microsoft Research, India.
  • 2nd Runner-up, Innovation Challenge, Johnson Controls, IIT Kharagpur.
  • 1st Runner-up, Prototype Demo Competition, IBM, Kharagpur.

2015

  • Head (Membership Development), IEEE Kharagpur Student Branch.
  • Special Mention, IEEE EMBS Pulse magazine, IEEE, USA.
